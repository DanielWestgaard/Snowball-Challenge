The worlds problem

World Animal Protection describes this the best: "Plastic waste is littering our oceans and threatening the lives of millions of marine animals. Seals, whales, dolphins, seabirds, fish, crabs and many other sea animals are dying and becoming sick because of this deadly environmental concern".

They continues to describe the danger behind this littering: "Sea animals often eat microplastics because of their small size. And plastic contains toxic chemicals, which can increase the chance of disease and affect reproduction. After ingesting microplastics, seals, and other animals, may suffer for months or years before they die. In 2014, an estimated 15 to 51 trillion microplastic particles were floating in the world’s oceans, weighing between 93,000 and 236,000 tonnes."

Why?

The reason we wanted to create this app was to help the world, the oceans and the animals living there. We see daily litter and garbage floating around in the oceans, just waiting to hurt the ecosystem or another animal.

That's why we created the app. Because we also knew that this is something we couldn't do alone. So we created this app! In order for others to help in the fight for animals to swim in cleaner waters again. As well as helping the oceans, we're also awarding the people that works the hardest. This way everyone will win!
